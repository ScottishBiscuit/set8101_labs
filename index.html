Introduction to Snake

Snake is a retro game which came about in the 1970s. It became popular after being featured on Nokia phones in the late 1990s.

Timeline of Snake's Evolution

  • 1976: The original arcade Snake game was released.
  • 1997: Nokia included Snake on its mobile phones, introducing it to a global audience.
  • Today: Snake is still popular in modern web-based versions.
